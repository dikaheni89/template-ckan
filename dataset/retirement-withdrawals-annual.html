About this dataset

Yearly amounts of monthly payout under Retirement Sum Scheme and CPF LIFE Scheme, and other retirement withdrawals (withdrawals made from age 55, property pledge, RS exemption etc).

Figures are rounded to the nearest hundred thousand dollars.
