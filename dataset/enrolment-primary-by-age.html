Example: Javascript »

A simple ajax (JSONP) request to the data API using jQuery.

var data = {
  resource_id: '0094016c-a6dd-4801-8608-329775e1dab3', // the resource id
  limit: 5, // get 5 results
  q: 'jones' // query for 'jones'
};
$.ajax({
  url: 'https://data.gov.sg/api/action/datastore_search',
  data: data,
  dataType: 'jsonp',
  success: function(data) {
    alert('Total results found: ' + data.result.total)
  }
});

Total Net Enrolment Rate for Primary And Secondary Education

Combined And Gross Enrolment Ratio For Primary, Secondary & Tertiary Education

Ministry of Education / 11 Jan 2023

Total number of students enrolled in a given level of education regardless of age expressed as a percentage of population in the theoretical age group for that level of education Refers to resident population, i.e. Singapore citizens and permanent residents. From 2005, the gross enrolment ratios include private education institutions. From 2010, the gross enrolment ratios are adjusted for residents who are based overseas and cannot be enrolled in Singapore. Year 1 Diploma courses are classified as post-secondary non-tertiary under ISCED 2011 from 2016 onwards.

Gender Parity Index For Primary, Secondary & Tertiary Students
