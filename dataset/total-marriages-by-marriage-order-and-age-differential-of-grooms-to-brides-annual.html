About this dataset

Total marriages comprise civil marriages registered under the Women’s Charter and Muslim marriages registered under the Administration of Muslim Law Act. They are obtained from records maintained by the Registry of Marriages and the Registry of Muslim Marriages.

Total may include marriages with attribute(s) not reported.
